Careers for Coding Bootcamp Grads

When you begin your coding journey, it's easy to overlook some of the careers that your new knowledge could bring you to.

Here's a short list!

  • Frontend developer: creates and manages code run by the browser
  • Backend developer: creates and manages code run by the browser
  • Fullstack developer: works on both the frontend and backend code
  • Sales engineer: uses their understanding of the codebase to find and recruit new clients, often answering client's questions about technical specifications the feasibility of modifications to fit their needs and
  • Customer support engineer: supports clients by acting as an intermediary between the client and the engineering team, translating between the two
  • Project manager: manages a team of engineers working on one or more projects

Regardless of which career you choose, it's important to be able to:

  • Communicate clearly about your code
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help!
  • Ask good, thoughtful questions
  • Be eager to learn what you don't know and eager to teach what you do know
